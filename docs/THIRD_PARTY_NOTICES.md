# Third-Party Notices

## Current App

The current macMender app does not ship Menu Bar management, the Thaw-port engine, the menu-bar scanner, or the menu-bar XPC helper.

The app still links against local macOS input/runtime APIs for its active Input, Dock previews, Window Switcher, permissions, and app control-center features.

## Historical Menu Bar Research

Historical Ice/Thaw menu-bar research and prior implementation notes were moved to `docs/archive/menu-bar-removed-2026-06-02/`. Those files are historical only and are not current product direction.

If Menu Bar management is rebuilt later, any copied or ported upstream source must preserve its original attribution and license notices.
